Battery Upgrade

LFP Battery: 150–200 km Range

Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) is the safest, most durable battery chemistry available. We retrofit any electric moped to run on LFP cells.

📏

Extended Range

Achieve 150 to 200 km on a single charge — ideal for day trips from Hội An to Đà Nẵng and back.

🛡️

Safer Chemistry

LFP cells are thermally stable and non-flammable — no risk of thermal runaway that plagues other lithium types.

⏳

3000+ Charge Cycles

LFP batteries last 5–10 years under normal use, far outlasting standard lead-acid or NMC packs.

⚡

Fast Charging

Full charge in 3–5 hours from a standard 220V outlet. No special equipment needed.

🔧

All Brands Supported

We upgrade VinFast, Yamaha, Honda, Peugeot, and other e-moped brands. Custom BMS programming included.

✅

Warranty Included

12-month warranty on all battery packs and installation. Free diagnostic checkup at 6 months.
